# 📰 Newsify

Newsify is a free iOS app that lets you read news from multiple sources in one place.

The app also includes AI-generated audio for articles, allowing you to listen instead of reading.

> **Status:** Beta

## Features

* 📰 Browse news by category
* 🔍 Search articles
* 🔖 Save bookmarks
* 🎧 AI-generated audio
* 🌙 Light & Dark Mode
* 💸 Completely free

## Requirements

* iPhone 15 or newer (required for AI features)
* iOS 26 or later

## Data Source

Newsify uses NewsAPI to retrieve articles from a variety of news publishers.

This project is not affiliated with or endorsed by NewsAPI.

## Built with

* Swift
* SwiftUI

## Beta

Newsify is still under development, so you may encounter bugs or unfinished features.
